Who we are:

The Woman + Gender Diverse Engineering Club is a student founded Camosun College club, that gives woman, trans, and non-binary engineering students a space to form community, advance their skillsets, and work on engineering projects designed to enhance society.

club founding values

  • Diversity Is Divity

    It all starts by learning how to focus on what you want.

  • Evolve together

    Next, youll reflect on what may be blocking you, and learn how to overcome these obstacles.

  • uplifting society

    The last step? We learn how to continually refine what weve learned. Think of this as your new beginning.
